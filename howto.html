Here is how to download the EndrrNation GDPS. Please note that if you do not own a legal copy of the official Geometry Dash, you wil practically be pirating GD. Please make sure you own a legal copy of the game on a supported trusted appstore. Want to go back to the list or the creator page? Scroll to the bottom.

1. Join the discord server here. You should land on the #welcome channel.

2. Go to the #info channel and react with the checkbox to gain access to the server. (Make sure to read the rules!)

3. Scroll down the category list until you find a category named ---ENDRRGDPS---

4. Go to the channel #gdps-downloads and download the file that applies to what device you play on.

5. After the application, click on the tools link provided in the downloads channel. You should be directed here.

6. Click on "registerAccount.php". You should be redirected here.

7. Fill in your account info as needed, however you do not have to use a real email to register, but it must follow an email adress template. (The demonstration is not of a real account.)

8. There is no extra verification required, just log into the registered account in-game and enjoy the EndrrNation GDPS!
